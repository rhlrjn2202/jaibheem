Dr. Mohanlal Patil

Dr. Mohanlal Patil (Born 05 August 1954) is an Indian politician from Madhya Pradesh. He is the leader of the Republican Party of India (A) currently, he is National General Secretary of Republican Party of India(A).

  • EX National President of Republican Party of India (A) (Since 2000-2009)
  • Student Leader (1972-1977) in Nagpur
  • Works at Republican Party of India(A) National Pesident(2003-2009)
  • National Executive Member atThe Buddist Society of India (since 2016)
  • Founder at Samta Sainik DAL (RPI- A),M.P.(since 2015)
  • Founder Member & Ex state President, M.P. at Dalit Panther of India (1979-1990)
  • Chairman at Dalit-Adiwashi Vanchit Forum(since 2015)
  • Chairman at Mahar Samaj Vikas Parisad(since 1993)
  • Chairman at Prabuddha Education Society(since 1993)
  • Editor&Publisher Republican Bharat (since 2009)
  • Ex state president at Bank of Maharashtra SC/ST/OBC Employess Association
  • Former President (M P) at All India ST/ST Bank Employees Federation
  • Went to Vidarbha Buniyadi High School at Nagpur (10 PASS)
  • Dr.Ambedkar College Diksha Bhumi Nagpur(B.COM 2)(1971-1973)
  • Studied Binzani mahavidhalaya at Nagpur university(B.COM)(1974-1975)
  • Studied C.P & Berar College Nagpur at Nagpur University( M.COM)(1975-1977)
  • Lives in Bhopal,Madhya Pradesh
