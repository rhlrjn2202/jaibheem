Organizing for Action What We Do?

Educational purposes: To establish Ambedkar research centres, High tech agricultural farm, medical & para-medical institutions and computer centres. To manage coaching centre for Employment assistance & Training centre for higher studies. The trust encourages Technical and Technological Institutions and to establish schools and colleges.
Social and charitable services: The trust should concentrate in conducting administration programs in Orphanage home, Children home, Old-age home and Hostels. It aims to administrate in giving employment opportunities, Youth fellowship, relief works and emergency blood donation group. It establishes an award function for the best social worker in every year. It administrates in Rehabilitation services, counselling centres & social welfare programs. The trust assists in visiting calamity areas & solid waste management schemes.
Economically up-lift of poor people: It worked in preventing and treating against in-taxinding & illicit alcoholism. The trust provides Ambulance services, food, medicines & clothes for needy people and helps infectious disease persons & fatal accident injuried people. It also establishes training centres for the candidates from poor families who are seeking self employment.
Skill development training activities and Digital India development works: The trust make contact and contract with different cultural centres in India & foreign countries. It conducts meeting with vedio coverage teams & to organise films for social development schemes. To establish music academies and to promote sports activities and construction of statues. It aims to make Ambedkar amusement high tech park and cultural centres.
